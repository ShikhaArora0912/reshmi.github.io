ABOUT ME

I live with my parents, grandparents and my older sister. I also have two cats, one is a grey tabby, his name is Raven and the other is a British Short Hair. Her name is Kitty. Raven is the cat in the picture to the far right and Kitty is the far left. I'm 15 years old and in Year 10 at Botany Downs Secondary College. I'm in Sir Peter Blake whanau which is associated with the color red. My option subjects this year were: 10ART, 10DIT and 10TRM. I don't know what I want to be when I grow up but may want to do something in IT.

My hobbies include art, netball, reading and baking. One of my option subjects is 10ART this year and I chose it because I love art and being creative. I play for one of the school netball teams and really enjoy it. Outside of school, I love being creative and that translates into mainly drawings, paintings and other crafts like origami. At home I like reading, my favourite genres of book are horror/psychological horror and crime. Some of my current favourite books I've read have been the series Once by Morris Gleitzman, Baby Teeth by Zoje Stage and You'd Be Home Now by Kathleen Glasgow.
