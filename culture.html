My nationality is New Zealander but my ethnicity is Indian. I was born and raised in New Zealand but both my parents were born and raised in India. Because of this I celebrate a lot of Indian traditions, some of those include: Holi, Diwali and Ganesh Chaturthi. Holi is a celbration of colours. People celebrate this by getting together and throwing different colors of powder at eachother. Diwali is the celebration of light. During Diwali we light small clay lamps and place them outside homes. Ganesh Chaturthi commemorates the birth of the god Ganesha. For this tradition, we pray and leave gifts for statues of Ganesh. I speak 2 languages fluently: Marathi and English because while growing up, we almost only spoke Marathi at home.
